# Contributing

External contributions are not accepted until KSwitch enables an approved DCO,
CLA, or no-external-contributions workflow for this repository.

Do not submit:

- secrets, tokens, private keys, or credentials;
- personal data;
- customer data;
- proprietary third-party code;
- code copied from a source that cannot be redistributed;
- production logs or regulated evidence.

Before external contributions are enabled, KSwitch must approve inbound terms
that preserve:

- Apache-2.0 licensing for SDK-client contributions;
- KSwitch's right to use, modify, commercialise, sublicense, and relicense
  Developer Edition/devkit/runtime contributions;
- contributor authority-to-contribute representations;
- third-party provenance certification.
