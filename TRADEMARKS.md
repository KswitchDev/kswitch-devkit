# KSwitch Trademark Guidelines

KSwitch, KSwitch DevKit, KSwitch Developer Edition, KSwitch logos, badges,
screenshots, product names, and trade dress are KSwitch marks.

## Permitted Nominative Use

You may use the KSwitch name in plain text to identify this repository, refer to
compatibility with KSwitch, or describe that your code uses a KSwitch SDK.

Examples:

- "uses the KSwitch Python SDK"
- "example policy for KSwitch DevKit"
- "compatible with KSwitch Developer Edition"

## Not Permitted Without Written Approval

You may not:

- name a fork, hosted service, product, package, or company in a way that implies
  it is official KSwitch software;
- use KSwitch logos, icons, screenshots, badges, or product artwork in marketing
  or product UI;
- claim partnership, certification, endorsement, "powered by", or official
  compatibility status;
- make production-readiness, security, compliance, performance, or benchmark
  claims involving KSwitch;
- remove trademark notices from redistributed source copies.

For permission, contact `trademarks@kswitch.io`.
