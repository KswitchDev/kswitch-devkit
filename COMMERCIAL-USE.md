# Commercial Use Boundary

KSwitch Developer Edition is for permitted local, non-commercial development,
education, demos, SDK integration, and bounded non-production evaluation only.

Commercial use requires a separate written agreement with KSwitch.

Commercial use includes production use, customer-facing use, managed-service
operation, resale, revenue-generating use, internal business operations,
competitive hosted-service operation, and use with customer or production data.

For commercial evaluation, use demo, synthetic, or throwaway data only. Do not
protect, monitor, govern, approve, deny, audit, report on, or automate real
employees, contractors, customers, services, systems, security incidents,
policies, approvals, transactions, or business processes.
