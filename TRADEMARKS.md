KSwitch names, logos, marks, and product names are not licensed except for
nominative identification of this repository and its origin.

Permitted nominative use:

- referring to the official KSwitch SDKs;
- referring to compatibility with KSwitch APIs;
- linking to KSwitch documentation or releases.

Prohibited use without written approval:

- fork or product names that imply official KSwitch status;
- logo use;
- badges such as "certified", "powered by", or "approved";
- partner or integration claims;
- screenshots or marketing copy that imply production support, SLA,
  certification, or commercial endorsement.
